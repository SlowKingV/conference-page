"Let the Music Speak!"

RETRO ELECTRIC MUSIC FESTIVAL 2022

The most anticipated music festival of all time, reuniting music fans from around the world to hear some of the most popular artists of the moment. You cannot miss it!

2022.10.15(SAT) ~ 16(SUN)

@ Grand Oasis Cancún, Cancún, México

Main Program

  • Conference

    Listen to music producers and aritst talk about their way to the top.

  • Exhibiton

    Appreciate the different instruments and merchandising from your favorite idols.

  • Forum

    Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with all the community online.

  • Workshop

    Try to produce your own music using the different sample some of our featured producers made specially for the event.

  • Retro Ignite

    Get opportunities to network with RE affiliates around the world, also after the festival.
